Caprese Salad

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1:Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter.
  2. Step 2:Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Step 3:Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Step 4:Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Step 5:Serve immediately for a refreshing and simple Caprese Salad.
