Italian Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough (yeast, sugar, warm water, flour, salt, olive oil)
  • Pizza Sauce (crushed tomatoes, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, pepper)
  • Toppings (mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, vegetables)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Step 1:Prepare pizza dough, let it rise.
  2. Step 2:Simmer pizza sauce ingredients, adjust seasoning.
  3. Step 3Roll out dough, transfer to pizza stone/baking sheet.
  4. Step 4:Spread sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  5. Step 5:Bake at 475°F (245°C) for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Step 6:Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
